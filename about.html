ABOUT US

Like you, we had no idea who to trust for travel advice.
That’s why we made TravelBug, a travel site written by real experts, not anonymous reviewers. Our writers are locals with hometown pride, parents who are road trip heroes, cruise junkies who know every ship at sea, and virtually everyone else in-between. As one of the top-10 travel information sites in the world as measured by comScore, a leading Internet measurement company, we have more than 50 writers—from lifetime locals to licensed tour guides—sharing useful travel advice and inspiration from destinations around the world. TravelBug has been honored by multiple awards since its inception, including the Eppy Awards, the W3 Awards, and the Communicator Awards. We give you the confidence to spend your vacation actually vacationing, not fumbling with a guidebook or second-guessing yourself.
